Costco Halal Lamb From New Zealand

Ingredients:

  • Sea Salt
  • Lamb
  • Canola oil
This is a pretty easy recipe, so I'm just going to get right into it. Because the lamb chops are fairly thick, you'll have to sear them in a cast-iron pan on the stove and then pop them in the oven. Two step cooking!
  1. Bring your lamb chops to room temperature.
  2. Preheat your oven to 350°F.
  3. Sprinkle all sides (top, bottom, sides, just not the bone-side) with Sea Salt
  4. Heat up a couple tablespoons of canola oil on an 8-10” cast iron pan.
  5. When the oil is glistening, it's go time.
  6. Repeat this searing process for each side of meat—top, bottom, left, and right.
  7. Serve! With some crispy greens like, I don't know, brussels sprouts!
